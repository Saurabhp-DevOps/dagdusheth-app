🙏 About the Temple

Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple is one of the most famous and revered Ganpati temples in Pune, Maharashtra. Devotees from all over India visit the temple to seek blessings.

🕰 Temple Timings

  • Morning: 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM
  • Aarti Times:
    • Kakad Aarti – 7:30 AM
    • Madhyan Aarti – 12:00 PM
    • Shej Aarti – 10:30 PM

📍 Location

Ganpati Bhavan, Budhwar Peth, Pune, Maharashtra – 411002

📋 Things to Know Before Visit

  • Maintain silence and follow temple rules
  • Mobile phones are usually not allowed inside
  • Footwear must be removed outside
  • Weekends and festivals are very crowded

🚫 Dress Code & Etiquette

  • Wear decent and traditional clothes
  • Avoid shorts or sleeveless tops
  • Respect devotees and temple staff

🌼 Best Time to Visit

Early morning or late night is ideal to avoid crowds. Ganesh Chaturthi festival attracts maximum devotees.
